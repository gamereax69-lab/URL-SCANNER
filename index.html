About ScanSafe

This URL+FILE Scanner is a simple and efficient tool designed to help you check whether any web link is safe before visiting it . It analyzes the URL structure and detects warning signs related to phishing, malware, and other online threats . With a fast, one-click scan, you can instantly verify a link without installing anything or sharing your personal data . Although no tool can guarantee complete protection, this scanner adds an important extra layer of safety, helping you avoid risky websites and browse the internet with greater confidence .
